Our Stance

Throughout our research, we have established our own collective stance on the presented questions.


Can AI produce authentic art?

We believe it can. AI is capable of producing art that is unique and desired. However, we argue that AI-generated art deserves to be appreciated within its own context. By this we mean that in the same way it is unfair to compare Travis Scott and Pablo Picasso, we feel AI produced art should be given its own contextual sphere.


Does the machine/algorithm deserve any credit?

We think right now, AI algorithms do deserve recognition in their role in the creation of art. However, in the future we envision a time when AI will be able to fully produce content on its own and will be able to learn from more than just what the developer gives it.


Will AI take our artistic and creative jobs?

We believe as long as humans find ways to work with AI, their jobs in creative industries will remain. AI can serve as a powerful tool, similar to the ones that already exist, and will hopefully push humans to create in a new and different way.
