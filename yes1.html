Of course, AI-generated art can't compete with human art.

But then how did an AI-generated portrait get auctioned for over $400,000?



Obvious Art, Edmond de Belamy, 2018, Ink on canvas

Created by a GAN algorithm, "Portrait of Edmond Belamy" was created using over 15,000 test images.


Obvious Art, La Comtesse De Belamy, La Duchesse De Belamy, Le Marquis De Belamy, Madame De Belamy, 2018, Ink on canvas


The auction house noted that the piece was “not a product of the human mind” but instead was “created by artificial intelligence, an algorithm defined by an algebraic equation.”

The selling of this piece begs the question of whether AI-generated content can really be thought of as authentic art.
